Ensalada de Coditos (Mexican Macaroni Salad)

Ingredients:

  • 10 ½ oz macaroni pasta
  • 1 ½ cup ham (diced)
  • 1 cup canned sweet corn (drained)
  • 1 cup pineapple (diced)
  • ½ cup carrots (shredded)
  • 2 Tbsp pickled jalapenos (chopped)

    • Dressing:

  • ¾ cup mayonnaise
  • ¾ cup Mexican crema (or sour cream or yogurt)
  • 2 Tbsp Yellow mustard
  • 3 Tbsp vinegar (from pickled jalapenos)
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni pasta and cook until tender.
  2. Drain pasta onto a large mixing bowl. Add 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise. Coat evenly and allow to cool down, stirring ocasionally as it cools.
  3. Add ham, corn, pineapple, carrots and pickled jalapeno on the bowl with pasta.
  4. Add mayonnaise, sour cream (or yogurt), vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper on top of veggies, then mix to coat well.
  5. Adjust with salt and pepper to your taste, cover the bowl and place it in the fridge until serving.
