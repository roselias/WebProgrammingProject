About Our Recipes:

Our Recipes was created as a fun way to fulfill the project requirements for the Web Programming class at the University of Houston-Downtown, and a place to put all of our favorite recipes into one place! Spanning different categories, from crockpot recipes to pastas and all the way to desserts, we believe there is something for everyone!

We hope you have half as much fun combing through, cooking and enjoying these recipes as we did compiling them! Thank you for stopping by!

About Us:

Our Recipes is built and maintained by Lourdes "Lola" Renteria, Christian Tuarez, and Chris Charles, all of them senior Computer Science students at the University of Houston-Downtown.
