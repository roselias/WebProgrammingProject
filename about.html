About Dish Delight:

Dish Delight was created as a fun way to fulfill the project requirements for the Web Programming class at the University of Houston-Downtown, and a place to put all of our favorite recipes into one place! Spanning different categories, from crockpot recipes to pastas and all the way to desserts, we believe there is something for everyone!

We hope you have half as much fun combing through, cooking and enjoying these recipes as we did compiling them! Thank you for stopping by!

About Us:

Dish Delight is built and maintained by Chris Charles, Lourdes "Lola" Renteria, and Christian Tuarez, senior Computer Science students at the University of Houston-Downtown.
